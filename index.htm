Soulecule

The soulcoin blockchain will power a decentralized digital trading card game.

Plan

In January of 2019, the interest rate will be reduced to 5%.

First I'll create a "pick up match" version of the game on the website. Then I'll run a soul-only coin offering for player/creator accounts. Players will trade soul for a variety of "glyphs" that will be used for creating cards. The initial website is a testing ground for the rules & scaling of the card game. Later the entire game system, along with the sums of bartered glyphs, will be transitioned onto an enhanced decentralized soul network.
